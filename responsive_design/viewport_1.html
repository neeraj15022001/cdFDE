Understanding ViewPort

  • Browser first renders on virtual view port,
  • which is wider than target device and
  • then shrinks this page to a point that it fits desired size.

  • Many pages are not mobile optimized and breaks when rendered at small width.
  • Browser attempt to make non mobile optimized website look better on mobile devices.
